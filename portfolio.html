About my basketball career:

From the age of 7 until 15, I dedicated myself to the sport, and becoming a professional basketball player was my ultimate goal. Throughout my journey, basketball has taken me to several countries, including Italy, Slovenia, Serbia, and Bulgaria. One of the highlights of my career was playing for the prestigious MZT Skopje Aerodrom basketball club.
